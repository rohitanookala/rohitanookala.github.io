Projects & Coursework

Throughout my time at Northeastern University, I've worked on projects spanning data science, full-stack development, and biochemistry research. Each one has pushed me to apply classroom knowledge to real problems and grow as both a developer and a scientist.

Modeling the Drivers of Movie Popularity

Analyzed a TMDB dataset of ~5,000 movies to identify which factors best predict popularity, including budget, runtime, and genre. Built linear and regression models in Jupyter Notebook, finding that budget and ROI explained 60–64% of popularity variation. Tested additional features to explore the importance of feature selection in predictive modeling.

ViTAL Hackathon — Digital Health Monitoring App

Served as Technical Lead and Backend Developer for a cross-platform React Native app that lets users scan nutrition labels and receive personalized dietary warnings based on health conditions and medications. Built a Django backend with ~10,000 product records from the Open Food Facts API, implemented Tesseract image recognition for label scanning, and designed user authentication all in under 12 hours.

Targeting BMP4 — Exploring Statins for Breast Cancer Metastasis

Designed and presented a scientific poster on the therapeutic implications of BMP4 in breast cancer metastasis. Analyzed ~50 preclinical experiments evaluating statins' effect on metastasis, contributing to drug-testing strategies and future research directions.

Anlitiks Inc. — Data Analyst Internship

Developed U.S. patient-count maps in R categorizing data across 50+ counties to support feasibility analysis for RapidAnalyzer, a platform that accelerates Real-World Evidence studies. Executed 25+ SQL queries in AWS Athena and investigated 5 AI tools for potential integration into the platform.
